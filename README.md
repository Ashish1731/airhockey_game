Air Hockey Game
A fully-featured Air Hockey game built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Play against an AI opponent with adjustable difficulty levels and customizable win conditions.

🎮 Features
Realistic Gameplay: Smooth physics with accurate paddle and puck collision

AI Opponent: Three difficulty levels - Easy, Medium, and Hard

Customizable Settings: Adjust AI difficulty and win score (3, 5, 7, or 10 points)

Visual Effects:

Particle explosions on hits and goals

Impact glow effects

Score pop animations

Goal celebration burst with text particles

Audio Feedback: Sound effects for paddle hits and goals

Responsive Design: Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices

Pause Functionality: Pause/resume gameplay with SPACE key or button


🛠️ Technologies Used
HTML5 - Structure

CSS3 - Styling with animations and responsive design

JavaScript (ES6) - Game logic, physics, AI, and rendering

Canvas API - 2D rendering for game graphics

🎯 How to Play
Control: Move your mouse or finger (on touch devices) to control your paddle on the left

Objective: Score points by hitting the puck into the AI's goal (right side)

Win Condition: First to reach the target score wins

Settings: Click the "Settings" button to adjust AI difficulty or win score

Pause: Press SPACE or click "Pause" to pause/resume the game

🎨 Game Features in Detail
AI Difficulty Levels
Difficulty	Speed	Error Margin	Reaction Delay	Description

Medium	0.22	12	6ms	Balanced challenge for casual play

Visual Effects
Impact Glows: Circular glow effects appear at collision points

Particle System: Colorful particles burst on paddle hits

Goal Celebration: "GOAL!" text particles with sparkle effects

Score Pop: Scores animate when a point is scored

Puck Trail: The puck leaves a trail for better visibility

UI Features
Side Score Displays: Large score numbers on both sides of the table

Center Scoreboard: Clean VS display at the top

Dark Overlays: Pause, Settings, Match Point, and Final Score overlays

Responsive Design: Adapts to any screen size
